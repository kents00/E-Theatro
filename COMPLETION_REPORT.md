## 📊 Audition Workflow

**Complete Status Flow Implemented:**

```
Student Registration
    ↓ Auto: Status = "Pending"
Admin Review
    ↓ Admin: Approve/Reject
Pending vs Approved
    ↓
Ready to Audition (Admin schedules date)
    ↓ Auto: Notification to student
Student Prepares
    ↓
Audition Date Arrives
    ↓
Student Auditions
    ↓
Admin Records Result
    ↓ Admin: Approve/Reject with feedback
    ↓ Auto: Notification to student
Final Status: Approved/Rejected
```
